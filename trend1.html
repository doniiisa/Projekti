Street Style Has Spoken!
Leather Midiskirts Are
Fall’s Top Trending Item

When seeking out the season’s hottest new trends,
the runways aren’t the only place to source sartorial
inspiration. The street style scene spotted in between
each show is equally as inspiring—it’s also just as telling
of fashion’s future. Among the top trends spotted during
the most recent fashion month, we couldn’t help but notice
a slew of sleek leather midiskirts worn time and again by
some of fashion’s chicest insiders. The embrace of shin-grazing
hemlines comes as no surprise, given that for the past few
seasons, many brands, like Bottega Veneta and Miu Miu,
have called upon the prim-and-proper quality of retro midi
silhouettes. For fall 2023 however, some leather midiskirts
err on the edgier side, courtesy of Cos, Marant Étoile,
Rag & Bone, while others are simply tailored-to-perfection.
